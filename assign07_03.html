Performing Arts

The arts programs at Hansen University are abundant and diverse as they are accessible. There are multiple opportunities to participate or be a patron to one of any number of plays, performance, exhibits, concerts, and film screenings. All this is available at no cost with your Student Activity Card. The arts should be available to all! Wether you are wanting to learn, increase your skill level, or simply enjoy another's artistic ability, come and join us!
